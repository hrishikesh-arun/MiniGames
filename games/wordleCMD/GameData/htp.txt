How to Play
The objective of the game is to guess the 5 letter word correctly.
If you get a letter that is in the word and in the correct position, It is signified by a # symbol.
If you get a letter that is in the word but not in its correct position, then it will be signified by a *
If you get a letter that is not in the word, then it will be signified by a X
You have only 6 guesses, after which the word will be revealed.
Hope you will find the game interesting. Enjoy!
